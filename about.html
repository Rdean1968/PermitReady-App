Our story

Built by construction professionals, for construction professionals

PermitReady was founded on a simple belief: finding the right team for a construction project shouldn't be so hard.

Why We Started PermitReady

After three decades in the construction industry, we saw the same problems over and over: homeowners overwhelmed by the process of finding trustworthy professionals, and skilled contractors wasting time and money chasing unqualified leads.

The typical homeowner has no idea where to start. They get bombarded by ads, read conflicting reviews, and end up choosing based on who calls back first — not who's actually the best fit for their project.

Meanwhile, contractors pay for "leads" that turn out to be tire-kickers, get undercut by lowball competitors on platforms designed to start bidding wars, and spend more time marketing than building.

PermitReady fixes this by connecting serious homeowners with vetted professionals before anyone else gets involved — and only charging when a project actually closes.
