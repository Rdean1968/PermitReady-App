Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information.

Effective: March 25, 2026
Last Updated: March 25, 2026

Our Commitment to Privacy

PermitReady ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform to connect homeowners with construction professionals in King and Snohomish County, Washington.

The Short Version
We collect only what we need to match you with the right people. We never sell your personal information to third parties. Your data stays between you, us, and the professionals you choose to work with.

Information We Collect

From Homeowners:

  • Contact information (name, email, phone number)
  • Project details (type, location, budget range, timeline)
  • Property address
  • Communication preferences

From Professionals:

  • Business information (company name, license number, years in business)
  • Contact information (name, email, phone, website)
  • Service areas and project types
  • Account credentials

Automatically Collected:

  • Device and browser information
  • IP address and general location
  • Pages visited and actions taken on our platform

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Match homeowners with qualified professionals
  • Facilitate communication between parties
  • Process transactions and manage escrow
  • Verify professional licenses and credentials
  • Send project updates and platform notifications
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

Information Sharing

We share your information only in these circumstances:

  • With matched parties: When a homeowner is matched with a professional, we share relevant contact and project information to facilitate the connection.
  • Service providers: We may share information with trusted vendors who help us operate our platform (payment processing, email services) under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government request.
We Never Sell Your Data
We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. Period.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (SSL/TLS)
  • Secure password storage
  • Limited access to personal information
  • Regular security assessments

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request that we correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your personal information
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at (866) 369-9698 during business hours.

Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Keep you logged in to your account
  • Remember your preferences
  • Understand how you use our platform
  • Improve our services

You can control cookies through your browser settings, though some features may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
