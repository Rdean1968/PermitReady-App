Section 1

Overview & Platform Description

PermitReady ("the Platform," "we," "us") is an online marketplace that connects homeowners planning construction projects with licensed professionals including general contractors, architects, interior designers, and structural engineers in King County and Snohomish County, Washington.

PermitReady is not a contractor, architect, designer, or engineer. We do not perform construction work, design services, or engineering services. We are a matching and communications platform only.

By registering for or using PermitReady, you agree to be bound by these Terms & Conditions in their entirety. If you do not agree, do not use the platform.

What PermitReady does
We review homeowner project briefs, match them with qualified professionals, facilitate secure communications between parties, and process the platform success fee through escrow when a project contract is signed. Once our fee is collected, PermitReady has no further involvement in or liability for the project.

Section 4

Deposit & Escrow Policy

When a professional chooses to claim a project lead on PermitReady, the following deposit and escrow terms apply:

4.1 Deposit amount. A $500 deposit is required to claim any project lead. This deposit is collected at the time of claiming and held in PermitReady escrow.

4.2 Deposit credit. The $500 deposit is not an additional fee. It is credited in full against the PermitReady success fee when the project contract is signed. You pay the success fee minus $500.

4.3 Escrow. All deposits are held in PermitReady's escrow account and are not commingled with PermitReady's operating funds.

4.4 Contact release. Upon payment of the deposit, the homeowner's full contact information — including name, email address, and phone number — is released to the claiming professional immediately.

How the deposit works — example
You claim a lead and pay $500 deposit → held in escrow
You agree on a $250,000 contract with the homeowner
PermitReady success fee: $250,000 × 1.5% = $3,750
Less deposit credit: $3,750 − $500 = $3,250 balance due
Homeowner's first payment flows through escrow → $3,250 deducted → remainder released to you
Section 5

Success Fee Structure

PermitReady charges a success fee only when a signed contract results from a platform introduction. No contract, no fee.

5.1 Fee calculation. The success fee is calculated as 1.5% of the total signed contract value between the professional and the homeowner.

5.2 Payment through escrow. The homeowner's first project payment is routed through PermitReady's escrow account. PermitReady deducts the success fee (less the $500 deposit credit) and releases the remainder to the professional immediately.

5.3 Fee finality. Once PermitReady's fee has been collected and funds released to the professional, PermitReady has no further financial interest in the project and no liability for any aspect of the project's execution, completion, or outcome.

5.4 No ongoing fees. There are no monthly subscription fees, no listing fees, and no fees of any kind beyond the $500 deposit and 1.5% success fee described above.

Section 6

Messaging & Communications Policy

6.1 Platform messaging required. From the moment a professional claims a lead until PermitReady's success fee is collected, all communications between the professional and the homeowner must be conducted through PermitReady's messaging system.

6.2 Thread ID tracking. Every project conversation is assigned a unique Thread ID (e.g., PR-0042-0018). All messages are logged with this Thread ID, timestamped, and archived. This record protects both parties and provides a complete project communication history.

6.3 Purpose of policy. The communications policy exists to protect homeowners, ensure professionals are compensated fairly for their work in winning a project, and maintain the integrity of the platform that both parties rely on.

6.4 After fee collection. Once PermitReady's fee has been collected, professionals and homeowners are free to communicate directly through any channel. PermitReady has no further involvement.

6.5 File sharing. Project documents, estimates, plans, drawings, and other files may be shared through the PermitReady messaging system. PermitReady is not responsible for the content, accuracy, or completeness of any files shared between parties.

Why this policy exists
PermitReady invests time and resources in sourcing, reviewing, and qualifying every homeowner project. The messaging policy ensures that professionals who benefit from this work compensate the platform fairly. It also protects homeowners by ensuring all early-stage communications are documented.

Section 8

Refund Policy

8.1 Refund eligibility. A professional who has claimed a project lead is eligible for a refund of their $500 deposit if no signed contract results from the introduction.

8.2 Refund process. To request a refund, the professional submits a refund request through the PermitReady dashboard. PermitReady reviews and processes approved refunds within 1-2 business days.

8.3 Processing fees. Refunds are issued for the full $500 deposit less payment processing fees charged by the payment processor (typically $5-15). These fees are not charged by PermitReady — they are charged by the payment network and are non-recoverable.

8.4 Platform credit. The payment processing fee amount is issued as a platform credit on the professional's PermitReady account. This credit is automatically applied to the deposit on the next lead claimed, ensuring professionals never permanently lose money on a failed deal.

8.5 No time limit. There is no time limit on refund requests. A professional may request a refund at any time after determining that a project will not result in a signed contract.

8.6 Ineligibility. Refunds are not available if the professional has already been paid under the escrow process (i.e., the project was won and the success fee was collected). Refunds are also not available if a Platform Integrity Fee has been assessed against the professional's account.

The credit guarantee
PermitReady commits to issuing a platform credit equal to any payment processing fees deducted from refunds. You will never permanently lose money on a deal that doesn't close.
